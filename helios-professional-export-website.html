About Helios Enterprises

Based in Andhra Pradesh, India, Helios Enterprises is a premium fresh vegetables exporter serving international markets with distinction. We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality Indian fresh produce to the UK, UAE, and Singapore.

Our commitment to quality begins at the farm level, working directly with certified farmers who follow best agricultural practices. Every product undergoes multiple quality checks, ensuring only the finest vegetables reach our international clients.

With FSSAI certification and APEDA registration, we maintain the highest standards of food safety and hygiene. Our modern cold storage facilities and efficient logistics network ensure that freshness is preserved from farm to destination.

Our Export Process

Seamless process from farm to your doorstep

1

Farm Selection & Sourcing

We work with certified farms across India, carefully selecting produce based on quality parameters and international standards. Regular farm visits ensure consistent quality.

2

Quality Inspection & Grading

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks. Products are sorted, graded, and cleaned using modern equipment and manual inspection for size, color, and quality.

3

Hygienic Processing

Processing in FSSAI-certified facilities maintaining strict hygiene standards. Temperature-controlled environment ensures freshness throughout processing.

4

Custom Packaging

Products are packed according to client specifications - mesh bags, carton boxes, or jute bags. Labels and documentation prepared as per import regulations.

5

Cold Storage & Logistics

Stored in modern cold storage facilities maintaining optimal temperature. Coordinated with reliable shipping partners for timely transportation.

6

International Shipping

Exported via air or sea freight based on product type and client requirements. Complete documentation and customs clearance support provided.
